Nummy Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Nummy ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal meal planning app. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password (stored securely via Supabase).
  • Meal preferences: Dietary restrictions, allergies, family size, and other preferences you provide.
  • Recipes and meal plans: Recipes you save and meal plans you generate.
  • Photos: Images you choose to scan for recipe extraction (processed by Anthropic's API and not stored).
  • Calendar data: If you connect Google Calendar, we read/write meal plan events only. We do not access other calendar events.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized meal plans using AI (Anthropic Claude).
  • To search for recipes matching your preferences (Spoonacular API).
  • To sync your meal plans to Google Calendar if you opt in.
  • To improve your experience across devices.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic — AI meal planning and recipe vision (privacy policy)
  • Spoonacular — recipe search (privacy policy)
  • Google — calendar integration (privacy policy)

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Nummy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@nummy.app (or update with your actual contact email).
