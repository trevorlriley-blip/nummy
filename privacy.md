# Privacy Policy for Nummy

**Last updated: March 2026**

Nummy ("the app") is a personal meal planning app. This policy explains what 
information is collected and how it is used.

---

## Information We Collect

**Account information**
When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name 
you choose. This is used solely to identify your account and personalize the app.

**Meal preferences**
During onboarding and through your profile, you may provide dietary restrictions,
allergies, health considerations, food preferences, and budget preferences. This
information is used exclusively to personalize your meal plans.

**Recipes and meal plans**
Recipes you save, meal plans you generate, and grocery lists are stored to provide
the app's core functionality.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- To generate personalized weekly meal plans using AI
- To sync your meal plan to Google Calendar (only if you connect your account)
- To remember your preferences between sessions
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties
- We do **not** use your data for advertising

---

## Third-Party Services

Nummy uses the following third-party services to function:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Supabase | Account authentication and data storage | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| Anthropic | AI-powered meal plan generation | [anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) |
| Spoonacular | Recipe search and data | [spoonacular.com/food-api/docs#Privacy-Policy](https://spoonacular.com/food-api/docs#Privacy-Policy) |
| Google Calendar | Optional calendar sync (only if you connect) | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

Your meal preferences and requests are sent to Anthropic's API to generate 
meal plans. No personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) 
is included in these requests.

---

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard 
encryption. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us (see below),
which will permanently remove all your data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Nummy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the 
top of this page.
